Evidhi: The AI Platform That Redefines Business Intelligence

How a college student turned a vision into an AI-driven revolution in customer intelligence

At the college, late one evening in my dorm room, I was battling an all-too-common frustration: inefficiencies in managing customer queries for small businesses trying to scale up. While brainstorming for a hackathon project, I realized that conventional systems were reactive, fragmented, and often left businesses guessing customer needs. That’s when the idea of Evidhi was born—a platform powered by AI that aims to redefine how businesses interact with their customers and analyze data for actionable insights.

Evidhi:InboxIQ Ai is a cutting-edge SaaS (Software as a Service) platform that allows clients to create a single,centralized chatbot that can be easily integrated into any website with a simple code snippet. This saves time and costs by eliminating the need for multiple chatbots. Additionally, it includes built-in chatbot training and offers real-time support.

The Birth of InboxIQ: The Brain Behind Evidhi

At the core of Evidhi lies InboxIQ, an advanced AI system that transcends being "just another chatbot." InboxIQ learns and evolves with every customer interaction across all platforms, creating a cohesive intelligence that helps businesses stay ahead. "I didn’t want to create a chatbot; I wanted a digital ally for businesses—one that never tires and consistently grows smarter," I explain. InboxIQ operates like an invaluable team member, offering seamless communication and business insights.

The breakthrough happened when I developed a system that integrates multiple AI models to function as a unified intelligence engine. Using cutting-edge technologies like Next.js 14, React 18, and OpenAI's latest APIs, Evidhi not only handles customer service but predicts customer needs, analyzes market trends, and delivers real-time business intelligence.

Real Stories, Real Impact

One of my early collaborations was with a local e-commerce startup. By implementing Evidhi's InboxIQ system, they reduced response time by 70% and identified a trend of rising demand for eco-friendly products, which they quickly capitalized on. Their sales tripled in just two months. Seeing such transformative results motivated me to push the platform’s boundaries further.

The Technical Brilliance Powering Evidhi

Evidhi's architecture leverages cutting-edge technologies to deliver unmatched performance:

  • Next.js 14 and React 18 for fast, interactive web experiences
  • OpenAI’s state-of-the-art models for natural language processing
  • Real-time data updates powered by Pusher
  • Secure authentication through Clerk
  • Seamless payment processing via Stripe

Unveiling Hidden Opportunities

Evidhi goes beyond solving problems; it uncovers trends and opportunities hidden in plain sight. For instance, another client, a boutique retailer, discovered a high volume of customer queries about personalized gifting options through InboxIQ’s insights. Acting on this, they launched a new service that became their most popular feature within weeks.
